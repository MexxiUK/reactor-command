00. System Overview

Welcome, Superintendent. You have been entrusted with the operation of the region's primary fission reactor grid.

Your mandate is threefold:

1. GENERATE

Produce MegaWatts (MW) to meet grid demand without causing meltdowns.

2. EXPAND

Use revenue to commission advanced reactor cores and build city infrastructure.

3. STABILIZE

Manage grid frequency and prevent brownouts during demand spikes.

Select a topic from the sidebar to access detailed operational parameters.

01. Reactor Operations

Core Mechanics

Reactors are the heart of your empire. Each unit converts fuel into Heat and Energy.

  • Heat: Byproduct of generation. 100% Heat = SCRAM.
  • SCRAM: Safety Control Rod Axe Man. An emergency shutdown that halts production until the core cools to 0%.
  • Overdrive (OVR): Manual override. Boosts output by 2.5x but generates heat rapidly.

03. The Network

The energy market is volatile. Prices ($/MW) update in real-time based on supply, demand, and external events.

STABLE MARKET

Standard operating conditions. Prices hover around base rates.

BULL MARKET

High Demand. Prices surge (~2.0x). Ideal time for aggressive Overdrive.

BEAR MARKET

Low Demand. Prices drop (~0.5x). Ideal time for cooling and repairs.

VOLATILE

Chaotic fluctuations. High risk, potential for massive gains or losses.

05. Corporate Contracts

Prove your mastery of the grid to earn Reputation and large cash bonuses.

STABILITY AUDIT

Maintain 100% Efficiency (No Brownouts) for the duration.
Fail condition: Power Drop.

SURGE TEST

Push Reactors to limits. Maintain >50% Heat.
Fail condition: SCRAM.

EXPORT RUSH

Divert power to the external grid. Export >50% of generation.
Tip: Shut down local city factories to pass.
