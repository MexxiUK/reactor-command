OPERATOR SAFETY & OPERATIONS HANDBOOK v1.0

1. Reactor Operations

Your primary duty is the management of the fission reactor grid. Units generate revenue via Energy Credits and power via MegaWatts (MW).

  • Heat Management: Reactors generate heat. If heat reaches 100%, the unit will SCRAM (emergency shutdown) to prevent meltdown. A SCRAMmed unit produces $0 and 0 MW until it cools.
  • Overdrive: You may manually override safety limits to boost output by 250% (2.5x). Use with caution. Excessive Overdrive leads to rapid heat accumulation.
  • Upgrades: Reactors can be upgraded from Gen II to Gen V (Fusion). Higher generations are cleaner, more stable, and produce significantly more power.

2. The Energy Market

Energy prices fluctuate based on global demand and weather events.

  • Stable: Normal operations. Price multiplier ~1.0x.
  • Bull Market: High demand (Heatwaves, etc.). Prices surge (~2.0x). Capitalize on this with Overdrive.
  • Bear Market: Low demand. Prices drop (~0.5x). Good time for maintenance/upgrades.
  • Volatility: Erratic price spikes. High risk, high reward.

3. Personnel

Hiring specialized staff provides passive bonuses to your entire grid. Staff costs increase exponentially ($1k -> $25k -> $1M -> $25M).

Engineer

+25% Power Output per staff.

Tax Consultant

+25% City Revenue per staff.

Safety Inspector

-30% Heat Generation. Critical for Overdrive.

Procurement

-10% Building/Unit Cost.

4. Contracts

Corporate offers special contracts for operators who demonstrate exceptional grid control.

  • Types: Stability Tests (No Brownouts), Surge Tests (High Heat), and Export Rushes (High Export).
  • Rewards: Contracts award large cash lump sums and Corporate Reputation.
  • Reputation: Higher reputation unlocks more lucrative contracts.
